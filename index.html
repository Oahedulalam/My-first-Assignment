Green Energy

Professor Muhammad Yunus was born on June 28, 1940, the third of 14 children, in Chittagong, Bangladesh. His father,a successful goldsmith, encouraged him to pursue higher education.After graduating from Dhaka University in Bangladesh, Professor Yunus received a Fulbright Scholarship to study Economics at Vanderbilt University in the United States, where he receiveda Ph.D. in 1971. While in the U.S.he also taught Economics at Middle Tennessee University. He returned to Bangladesh upon its independence in 1972 to serve as the Head of the Economics Department at the University of Chittagong.
